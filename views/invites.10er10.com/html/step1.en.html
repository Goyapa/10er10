What happens ?

You are invited to the 10er10 project. 10er10 is a web platform to store and listen music from the web, directly through a browser.

What makes 10er10 unique

Community

On 10er10, songs you store on the website are directly accessible to the other members.
Once your song is uploaded, you have to review it, which means to check that the meta-data (title, artist, album...) are correct. The 10er10 catalog accuracy directly depends on the meta-data accuracy.

Technology

10er10 uses new HTML5 technologies available on Firefox and Google Chrome browsers. Drag'n'drop, audio playback without third party plugins, and more : 10er10 is a pionner HTML5 web application.

Anonymous

10er10 don't store your email address, or the IP address you're using to connect.
